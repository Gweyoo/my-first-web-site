What is Nutrition?

Even when you train hard as you can and destroy your muscles properly, it won't grow as much when you do not give yourself a proper Nutrition that your body needs. There are Major nutritions called Protein, Carbohydrate and Fat. Protein helps with your muscle building and muscle recovery and Carbohydrate provides energy for your body to excercise or go throughout your day!

There are a lot of areas of excercise and different intensity of excercise. It all depends on your body parts and your recovery speed and your genetic.
